What the skills changed

Change Reason Source
Serif → Geist uppercase wordmark Serif very discouraged as default. Sans display is the right choice unless genuinely editorial/luxury. TasteSkill §4.1
Background: #090909 → #050505 OLED Ethereal Glass archetype: deepest OLED black base. High-End Visual §3.A.1
Double radial mesh gradient Two overlapping radial gradients at different positions + opacities for depth. High-End Visual §3.A.1
Film grain overlay (opacity 0.015) Subtle SVG noise texture on fixed pseudo-element — physical paper/OLED feel. High-End Visual §3.A.2
Double-Bezel on cards + input Outer shell (1px padding, subtle bg) + inner core (blur, border, inset shadow). Machined hardware feel. High-End Visual §4.A
Narration: light Geist (weight 300) Expressive weight play within same family. No mixed-family emphasis. TasteSkill §4.1 emphasis rule
Phase pill: nested (outer shell) Double-Bezel even on tiny elements. Outer 0.5px padding ring. High-End Visual §4.A

In code: The double-bezel is subtle — just padding: 0.5px on an outer wrapper with a slightly different bg, then the real card inside with rounded-[15px] inside rounded-[16px]. The grain is a fixed SVG filter overlay, pointer-events-none. The radial meshes are two divs positioned absolutely.

Motion (not shown in static): Cards enter via Motion whileInView — fade up from translate-y-12 with blur dissolving. Buttons use custom cubic-bezier(0.32, 0.72, 0, 1). Send button active: scale-[0.97].
